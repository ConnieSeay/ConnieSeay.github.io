Once the screenshot has been taken, a notification will appear on the lower right corner of your screen. Click on the notification and then select the Copy to Clipboard option. This will copy your screenshot onto the clipboard so you can paste it into your preferred image editing program.

To save your screenshot, open the image editor and paste the screenshot into the program. Once the image is open, click on the File menu and then save the image as a .PNG file. This will retain the original quality of your screenshot.

Finally, you can print the image from the image editor. Select the Print option from the File menu and then choose your desired printer, paper size and other printing settings. Once the image is printed, you are ready to share your screenshots!
