Q1. How can I take a screenshot on my iPad Pro 11?

A1. To take a screenshot on your iPad Pro 11, press and hold the volume up button (on the right side) and the power button (on the top) simultaneously until you hear the screenshot sound. Your iPad Pro 11 will save the screenshot to the Photos app.

Q2. How do I find the screenshot I just took?

A2. To find the screenshot you just took, open the Photos app. Your screenshot will be at the end of your Camera Roll.

Q3. How can I edit or delete a screenshot?

A3. To edit or delete a screenshot, open the Photos app and select your screenshot. Tap the “+” icon in the upper-right corner of the screen and tap “Edit”, or tap the trash can icon in the upper-right corner to delete the screenshot.

Q4. How can I take more than one screenshot at a time?

A4. To take more than one screenshot at a time, press and hold the volume down button (on the left side) and the power button (on the top) simultaneously until you hear the screenshot sound. Your iPad Pro 11 will take multiple screenshots in succession and save them all to the Photos app.

Q5. How do I share my screenshots with someone else?

A5. To share a screenshot with someone else, open the Photos app and select the screenshot you want to share. Tap the share icon (box with an arrow pointing upwards) to the left of the trash can icon in the upper-right corner of the screen. Then, select which sharing method you would like to use (for example, Messages, Mail, AirDrop, etc.).
