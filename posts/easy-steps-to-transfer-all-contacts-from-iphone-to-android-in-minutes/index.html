Here are some simple steps that will help you smoothly transfer all contacts from an iPhone to an Android phone. First, make sure that you have a Google account. Enter your Google account information and make sure that Contacts is on. Third, on your Android device, open the Contacts app. Go to the Settings tab and select Manage Contacts. Choose the option to Import Contacts and select your Google Account. Fourth, your contacts should now be synced between devices. Now you have successfully transferred all contacts from your iPhone to Android device!
