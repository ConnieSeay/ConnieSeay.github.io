Then, wait for a six-digit or ten-digit code to appear on your iPhone. Once you have the code, enter it on your Android device which you are transferring from.

Next, wait for the Transfer Data screen to appear. This window offers you an option to select the data you would like to transfer from your source Android device. Once you have made your selection, tap on ‘Next’. Lastly, the transfer process will begin and you will see a progress bar showing the progress. When the progress bar is completed, your data transfer is completed.
