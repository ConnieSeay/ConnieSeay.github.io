Q2. How do I take a screenshot on my Apple iPhone 12 mini? A2. To take a screenshot on your Apple iPhone 12 mini, press the “Side” button and the “Volume Up” button at the same time, and release. The screenshot will be saved to the Photos app, where you can view, edit, and share it.

Q4. Can I delete screenshots? A4. Yes, you can delete screenshots from your Apple iPhone 12 mini. To delete a screenshot, open the Photos app, select the screenshot, and tap the Trash icon.
