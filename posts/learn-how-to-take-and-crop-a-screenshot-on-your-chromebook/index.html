Taking a Screenshot on Chromebook#

Taking and cropping screenshots on a Chromebook is a very useful feature if you need to create an image of the screen for a presentation or project. It’s simple to do and can be done quickly with a few clicks. Here’s how to take and crop screenshots on a Chromebook.

First, press the Ctrl and Window Switcher (F5) keys at the same time. This will capture the entire screen and save it to your Downloads folder. You can also press Ctrl + Shift + Window Switcher (F5) if you only want to capture the currently active window.

To crop the screenshot, open the image with the Gallery application on your Chromebook. Select the crop tool and then drag the cursor around the area you wish to keep. Click the checkmark when you’re done to save the cropped image.

Finally, if you want to print or share the screenshot, you can right-click on the image in the Gallery and choose the correct option for your needs. From there, you can save the screenshot as a jpeg, png or other file format.

Taking and cropping screenshots on a Chromebook is easy and can help you quickly capture images of your screen. With just a few clicks, you can capture and crop the perfect screenshot for your needs.
