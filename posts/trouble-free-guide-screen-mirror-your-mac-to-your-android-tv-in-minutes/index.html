1. Cara Mengarahkan Layar Mac ke TV Android#

Mirroring your Mac display to an Android TV couldn’t be easier with the right set of instructions. In just a few minutes you can replicate your Mac’s screen onto your Android TV. To begin, you need an Apple TV. Without it, you won’t be able to mirror your Mac display to your Android TV. You should then pair the Apple TV and Android TV by setting up a network connection, or attaching them directly to each other with a cable. Once connected, you can open your Mac’s display settings and select the Apple TV as an output option. This should then allow your Mac’s display to appear on your Android TV. Finally, ensure your Mac’s sound settings are adjusted correctly so that any audio plays through the TV’s speakers. You can now watch movies, play games, and view photos stored on your Mac on the big screen of your Android TV!

First, make sure both the Mac and the Android TV are connected to Wi-Fi. Next, open the Mac’s quick action menu and select “Screen Mirroring”. Finally, select the Android TV from the list of available devices and the Mac screen will be mirrored to the TV in just a few moments.
