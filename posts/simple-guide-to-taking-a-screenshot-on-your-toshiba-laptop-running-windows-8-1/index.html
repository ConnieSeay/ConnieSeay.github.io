1. Introduction to How to Take Screenshots on a Toshiba Laptop Running Windows 8.1#

Taking screenshots on a laptop can be an easy task when you know how, and this guide will help you do just that! This guide will focus on Toshiba laptops running Windows 8.1. To take a screenshot on your Toshiba laptop, all you have to do is press the Print Screen (often abbreviated as PrtSc) button on your keyboard. This will copy the screenshot to your clipboard. You can then open Paint (Windows’ built-in image editing application) and press Ctrl + V to paste the screenshot. From here, you can edit the image as you please and save it to your laptop. Alternatively, you can also press the Windows + PrtSc key combination to save the screenshot directly to your computer. With this method, you can decide which folder you’d like to save the screenshot to. It’s important to note that this method only works on Windows 8.1. That’s all there is to it! Now you’re ready to take screenshots on your Toshiba laptop running Windows 8.1.

If you want to take a partial screenshot, you can also press the Alt and Print Screen keys at the same time. This will capture only the active window and store it in the clipboard. To access the Snipping Tool, type snipping tool in the search bar. From here you can choose whether to capture a rectangular snip, free-form snip, window snip, or full-screen snip. Once you take a screenshot, you can save it as a JPEG, PNG, GIF, or HTML file.
