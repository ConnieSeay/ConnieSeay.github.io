A 3. To disconnect your AirPods Pro from your Android phone, open the Settings and then open Bluetooth. Select the AirPods Pro from the list and tap on the Disconnect button. Then the AirPods Pro will be disconnected from your Android phone.

A 4. Yes, you can use your AirPods Pro with other devices such as laptops, tablets, and even other phones. To do this, you need to pair the AirPods Pro with the device you want to use them with.
