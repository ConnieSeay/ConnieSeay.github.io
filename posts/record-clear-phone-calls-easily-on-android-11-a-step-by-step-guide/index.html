2. Steps to Record Calls on Android 11#

Call recording is one of the most useful features of a smartphone. With the release of the latest Google mobile operating system, Android 11, the ability to record calls comes with a few extra features. The process is easy and efficient, so you can quickly capture important conversations. Here’s a quick tutorial on how to record a phone call on Android 11.

First, you’ll need to enable call recording on your device. Head over to the Settings app and tap on Calls. Here, you’ll find an option to Record Calls. Toggle this switch to enable the feature. Once call recording is enabled, you can start recording your conversations.

During a call, tap on the Record icon. This is usually represented by a red circle icon with a dot in the middle. Once you press the icon, the call will start recording. You can tap the record icon again to stop recording.

All recorded calls will be saved in the Recorded Calls section of the Phone app. Here, you can listen to the conversation and even share it with your contacts. To delete a call recording, simply select the item and press the delete button.

With Android 11’s call recording feature, you can capture important conversations quickly and easily. Be sure to remember to enable it through the settings as this is a necessary step for recording calls on your device.
