1. Learn the Basic Steps of Taking Screenshots#

Taking screenshots is a great way to capture what is happening on your Samsung Laptop with Windows 11. If you are new to the world of Windows 11, it can be a bit confusing to know where to start. Fortunately, taking screenshots is a relatively simple task. Here are the basic steps to get you started:

First, navigate to the page that you wish to take a screenshot of. Once there, press the ‘PrtScn + Fn’ button on the keyboard. This will capture the entire page and it will be stored in a temporary file. To save the screenshot, open the ‘Paint’ application, select ‘Ctrl+V’ to paste the image, adjust its size if necessary, and click ‘Ctrl+S’ to save it as an image file.

If you require a shot of a specific area of the window, press ‘Alt + PrtScn + Fn’. This will capture the current window, including any open menus or dialog boxes. Paste the image in the same way as before and save it.

The screenshot will be saved in the ‘Pictures’ folder as a PNG file, which can be easily shared with others. You can also modify the image or incorporate it into documents, presentations and more.
